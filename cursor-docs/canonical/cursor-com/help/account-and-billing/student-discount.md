# Student discount

Cursor offers a free year of Pro for eligible students.

## Who is eligible for the student discount?

You must have a **.edu email address** and an individual Cursor account (not a team member).

## How do I apply for the student discount?

1. Sign in to Cursor with your .edu email address
2. Go to [cursor.com/dashboard](https://cursor.com/dashboard)
3. Find the **Student Verification** section
4. Complete verification through SheerID (Cursor's third-party verification partner)

## What if I'm a student without a .edu email?

Only .edu email addresses qualify. There are no exceptions for other academic email domains.

## What if SheerID can't verify me?

Verification issues should be directed to [SheerID support](https://www.sheerid.com/), as they manage the verification process independently.

## Can I transfer my student discount to another account?

No. Student verification is one-time per email and cannot be transferred.

## What happens after the student discount year ends?

Your Pro subscription continues at the regular $20/month rate unless you cancel.
