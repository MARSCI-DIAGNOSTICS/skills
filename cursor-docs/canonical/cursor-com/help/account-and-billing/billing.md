# Billing and payments

Manage your subscription, payment methods, and billing details from your [dashboard](https://cursor.com/dashboard).

## How does billing work?

Your subscription renews monthly or annually, depending on the plan you chose. The cycle starts on the date you first subscribed.

Each plan includes a set amount of AI usage. If you go over, you'll see a notification in the editor. Check your usage on the [dashboard usage tab](https://cursor.com/dashboard/usage).

## How do seats work on a Teams plan?

Teams accounts charge per seat; one seat per team member.

- When you add a member mid-cycle, you're charged for the remaining time in that period
- If a member has used credits and is removed, their seat stays occupied until the billing cycle ends
- Team admins manage seats through the [dashboard](https://cursor.com/dashboard)

## Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?

**Individual plans (Pro, Pro+, Ultra):**

1. Go to [cursor.com/dashboard/billing](https://cursor.com/dashboard/billing)
2. Click **Upgrade Now** on the green banner at the top

**Teams plan:**

1. Go to [cursor.com/dashboard](https://cursor.com/dashboard)
2. Click **Billing and Invoices** in the left sidebar
3. Click the **Upgrade Now** button to switch to yearly billing

## How do I update my payment method?

1. Go to [cursor.com/dashboard](https://cursor.com/dashboard)
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. This opens the Stripe billing portal where you can add a new payment method, update your existing card, or set a default

Cursor accepts all major credit and debit cards through Stripe. You can only remove a payment method after your subscription has been fully canceled and the billing period has ended.

## How do I update my billing information?

Update your company name, address, and tax information through the billing portal. Changes only affect future invoices; we cannot modify historical invoices.

## Where can I find my invoices?

All billing history is available in the billing portal. View and download current and past invoices. Invoices must be downloaded manually; automatic invoice emails are not available yet.

## What if my billing issue isn't covered here?

Email [hi@cursor.com](mailto:hi@cursor.com) from the email address linked to your account. Include your account details and a description of the issue.
