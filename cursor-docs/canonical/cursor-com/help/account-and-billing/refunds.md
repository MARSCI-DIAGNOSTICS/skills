# Refund policy

When and how to request a refund for your Cursor subscription.

## Can I get a refund?

In most cases, no. Refunds may be available in certain circumstances, such as accidental upgrades or billing errors. Email [hi@cursor.com](mailto:hi@cursor.com) and the team will review your case.

On-demand usage and previous billing periods are not eligible for refunds.

## Can I cancel without a refund?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time to stop future charges. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
