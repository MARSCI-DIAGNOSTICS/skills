# Invoices

Download current and past invoices from your billing dashboard.

## Where are my invoices?

1. Go to [cursor.com/dashboard](https://cursor.com/dashboard)
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. This opens the Stripe billing portal
4. Scroll down to view your billing history
5. Click on any invoice to view or download it as a PDF

Invoices must be downloaded manually for now.

## Can I update my billing information on future invoices?

Yes. You can update your company name, address, and tax/VAT information through the Stripe billing portal. Changes apply to future invoices only; previously issued invoices cannot be modified.
